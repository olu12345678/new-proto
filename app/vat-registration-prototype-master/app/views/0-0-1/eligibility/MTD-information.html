VAT is changing

The new ‘Making Tax Digital’ (MTD) system will change the way some companies have to report their VAT.

Making Tax Digital (MTD) for VAT

From 1 April 2019, any company that makes more than £85,000 in VAT-taxable sales over a 12-month period must use compatible software to:

  • keep digital VAT records
  • send VAT Returns
