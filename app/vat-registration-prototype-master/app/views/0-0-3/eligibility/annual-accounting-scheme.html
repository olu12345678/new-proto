Is SWALES LTD applying for the Annual Accounting Scheme?

This scheme is an option for companies with an estimated VAT-taxable turnover of £1.35 million or less.

Companies on the scheme:

  • submit one VAT Return a year, rather than quarterly or monthly returns
  • make monthly or quarterly payments, based on an HMRC estimate of their end-of-year VAT bill

It may not suit companies that:

  • want to keep up to date with the exact amount of VAT they owe or need to reclaim
  • regularly reclaim more VAT than they charge, because they will only get one VAT refund a year
