Making Tax Digital for business

Most VAT registered businesses with a taxable turnover above £85,000 must use compatible software to keep VAT records and send VAT returns.

If your taxable turnover is above, or will go above, £85,000 in any rolling 12 month period, you must sign up for Making Tax Digital for VAT.

Register for VAT before you sign up for Making Tax Digital

If your taxable turnover is below £85,000 in any rolling 12 month period, you can sign up voluntarily.
