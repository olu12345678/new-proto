In any 12-month period has the business gone over the VAT-registration threshold?

£85,000 is the current VAT-registration threshold. It is the amount of VAT-taxable sales the business can make before it has to register for VAT.

What to include as VAT-taxable sales?

Assume the business is already VAT-registered. Include the sale of all goods and services that are not exempt from VAT. You must include goods and services that have a 0% VAT rate.

Find out more about which goods and services are exempt from VAT (opens in a new tab).
