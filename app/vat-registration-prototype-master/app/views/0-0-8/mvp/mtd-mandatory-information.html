You must sign up for Making Tax Digital for VAT

Most VAT registered businesses with a taxable turnover above £85,000 must use compatible software to keep VAT records and send VAT returns.

As your taxable turnover is above, or will go above, £85,000 in a rolling 12 month period, you will sign up for Making Tax Digital for VAT as part of the registration process.
