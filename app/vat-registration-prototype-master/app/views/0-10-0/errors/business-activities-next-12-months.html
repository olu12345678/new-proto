The business must register for VAT using a different service

If the business:

  • does all of its business outside the UK or has its head office outside the UK - use the online VAT registration service
  • is selling assets that you have claimed a repayment of VAT under Directive 2008/9 or 13th Directive refund arrangements - register using form VAT1C
  • is selling goods located in Northern Ireland at the time of sale - use the online VAT registration service
  • is selling or moving goods from Northern Ireland to an EU member state - Use the other VAT registration service
  • is bringing goods into Northern Ireland from an EU member state - register using form VAT1B
  • is selling goods into Northern Ireland from an EU member state - register using form VAT1A
