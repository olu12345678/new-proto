The business must register for VAT using a different service

If the business:

  • is selling or moving goods from Northern Ireland to an EU member state - register using form VAT1A
  • is bringing goods into Northern Ireland from an EU member state - register using form VAT1B
  • is selling assets that it bought from a country outside the EU and claimed a VAT refund on - register using form VAT1C
  • does all of its business outside the UK or has its head office outside the UK - Use the other VAT registration service
